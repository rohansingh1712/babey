How to Play

Goal: Drive your car 🚗 from home to the grocery store 🏪 through the maze

Move: Use arrow keys to navigate through the maze

Fires: Fires 🔥 spawn randomly every 1-10 seconds at levels 1-5. They level up every 5 seconds if ignored.

Extinguish: Click anywhere to activate your water bucket 🪣 (lasts 5 seconds). Click and hold directly on fires to put them out. Higher level fires take longer (Level 1: 2s, Level 2: 3s, Level 5: 6s). Your progress persists—refill your bucket and continue where you left off!

Visual Feedback: Fires fade as you extinguish them. Each fire level also reduces your maze and car visibility by 30%. At level 4+, you're nearly blind!

Time: You have 1 minute per day. If time runs out, you sleep and wake up to try again from where you left off.

Days: Each new day is a fresh attempt. Your maze position carries over until you reach the store.
